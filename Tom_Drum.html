Tom-Tom Drum

The Tom-Tom Drum was invented by Ludwig in 1922.



The Tom-Tom drum is an indispensable part of the drumset.



The Tom-Tom helps bring color, drama, and excitement to the performance.



They are mounted to the bass drum or on a trap table.
